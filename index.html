Approaches Offered

Neuro Linguistic Programming

Neuro Linguistic Programming (N.L.P.) looks at our patterns of behaviour, what we do internally to keep those patterns running, how we can make changes within the patterns to bring about a different behaviour and result.


Read more

Clinical Hypnotheraphy

Clinical Hypnotheraphy is a gentle relaxing way to access the underlying processes that motivate and generate our thoughts and behaviours. With 'negative' thoughts or behaviours it allows us to re-align ourselves to think and behave in a more positive way.

Counselling

About

New Perspective

New Perspectives was established in 1994. The aim was to offer clients brief, effective and varied approaches to their difficulties That aim remains the same today. In our clinics everyone can expect to be treated with care, fairness, understanding and respect. No matter what the difficulty, there is a solution.

Aurelie Silverlock

I began working as a therapist in 1994 and continue to do so today. I have worked with people as they've changed their jobs, financial situation; health status; weight; relationships; communication skills; beliefs about themselves and their world; destructive behaviours; limiting attitudes. It is a privilege to be a part of someone's life as they negotiate their way through what may seem a difficult and challenging time.

I was trained here in Ireland, UK and USA. All of my training is based on the idea that everyone has the capability and the knowledge to live their lives excellently. My job is to bring that capability and knowledge to the fore and to enhance it.


I am there to support, to help uncover new options or redesign what is already in place to work to better effect for all involved. Each day my clients teach me something new and I appreciate that. I am very grateful and delighted to be able to say I love my job.

Aurelie Silverlock - Member in Practice I.I.C.H.
