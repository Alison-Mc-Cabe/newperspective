Gone For Good

Gone For Good

Using tried and tested techniques to change your thoughts to alter your behaviour, Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP) &Clinical Hypnosis for 10 weeks will bring you excellent results and the ability to train your brain 'to think like a thin person.


Ask yourself the following . . .

Do you want to take control of your weight?

Do you have to make immediate changes for your health's sake?

Do you want to have the figure that shows you are healthy, vibrant and confident?

Do you want to STOP negative, destructive eating and living patterns?

Do you want to STOP the feelings and thoughts that sabotage your efforts?

Do you want to make these changes permanent?

Do you want your excess weight GONE FOR GOOD?

If the answer is YES to any or all of the above then read on . . .

HOW DOES IT WORK?

It works by showing you HOW to follow a weight loss programme properly without turning to food for comfort. You'll learn how to avoid cheating. You'll learn how to resist tempting food. You'll learn how to motivate your self to exercise, even if you are not naturally inclined to do so.

Once you know how to do these things You'll step on the scales and see a lower number reflected week after week. You'll drop clothing sizes. You'll have more energy, more confidence, better health, improved self-esteem.

WHAT DO YOU DO?

Every day for 10 weeks you will have a task to carry out. Some will be once-offs. Others will be repeated. We meet to discuss how you will approach the tasks and what you may have to alter to allow you carry them out.

We will look at sabotaging thinking and EFFECTIVE responses to it. We will look at feeling deprived, discouraged and stressed due to other life events. As a result of this work You'll not only overcome your current difficulty, you will also have the skills to overcome any future difficulties.

It works by showing you how to cope with:

  • cravings
  • hunger
  • stress
  • strong negative emotions
  • low motivation

DOES IT WORK?

YES it does. I've used it myself and continue to do so daily.

WILL IT WORK FOR ME?

YES it will. The harder you work at the programme the better your results will be. It is a commitment to you, the programme and your future so think about it carefully. Done properly this will be the last time you will tackle your weight issues.

By committing to making the changes in this programme you will be giving yourself the mental and emotional foundations you need to succeed NOW and in the FUTURE when your excess weight has GONE FOR GOOD

I have been working in the area of Behavioural Change with people since 1993. I understand the challenges this sort of commitment to change can bring. I promise to work as hard as you do. We will have 8 face-to-face sessions during the programme.

I will support you in every way possible HOWEVER I am not soft. I am tough but fair. I know ALL the excuses. I have made them to myself and others over the years. All your materials i.e. task lists, cards, notebooks are supplied and there is a follow-on session 2 months after completion.

I want this to work for you for once and for all. I want your weight GONE FOR GOOD. . . . . . don't you?
