Courses

Introduction to NLP

A weekend workshop introducing N.L.P.

NLP is a powerful yet gentle approach to overcoming life's problems. It offers ways to enhance self-esteem, improve relationships, it assists with the creation of positive motivation, independence and promotes self healing.





Traditionally used for excellence in business and sport, this workshop focuses on NLP as a therapeutic tool and highlights its value to those in the support services such as therapists, life coaches, teachers, health professionals and complementary practitioners.

Our aims for this weekend are:
To introduce the solutions that NLP has to offer to a wide range of difficulties. Like so many things, change is easier when you know what to do.

NLP Practitioner Training Certification

Take your skills to the next level.

Become exceptional in your field whether you are a worker, manager, therapist, teacher, trainer, coach or an individual looking for improvement. You will learn a set of skills that can be the foundation for reaching all the goals in your life.





We will take you to the roots of understanding behaviour. Before NLP we had explanations as to why we act in certain ways. Since NLP we have the understanding of how we act in those ways.

Throughout this training you will uncover and work with the processes that precede behaviour. You will learn the very fabric of thought and action. It is said that NLP has made the greatest contribution to the behavioural sciences in the last 30 years.

Master Practitioner Training

Master the creative & innovative application of N.L.P.

A Master Practitioner of N.L.P. is an individual who masters the creative as well as the innovative application of the principles and methodology of N.L.P. as well as using the standard N.L.P. techniques.





You will learn the principles of N.L.P. and how to create your own techniques. You will refine and expand on the skills and techniques you acquired during your Practitioner training.
You will be able to apply the advanced patterns of N.L.P. learned in this training to your on going personal development, your profession or business and to develop interventions for any context.

Re-source Yourself

Find the balance that makes your life work.

Are you in your 30's, 40's, 50's 60's and asking 'Where is it'? The fun, the great job, the fitness, the good health, the dream home, the family, the money? The answers could lie in finding the balance that makes your life work best for you.





RE-SOURCE YOURSELF is a course run over a 10 week period with sessions lasting just over an hour. It can be done individually or within a group setting.
